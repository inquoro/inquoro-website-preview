Our Mission

Tracing the Curve

Opinion doesn't shift in straight lines. It arcs — forcefully bent by turmoil or gently reshaped by generational drift and the slow erosion of old certainties.

Tracing that arc requires observation that is careful enough to detect consensus forming, dissent emerging, or assumptions quietly falling away.

Inquoro exists to do this work: to listen with precision, to measure with rigor, and to turn what is heard into understanding that guides.

Our Panels

The Sources Of Discovery

Inquoro reaches people through multiple pathways, each built for a different research need. Projects draw from one source or combine several.

Signal Field

The National Panel

A growing, opted-in panel of verified participants — recruited directly, stewarded over time. Signal Field members are real people in an ongoing relationship with research, not anonymous respondents or bots chasing incentives.

The panel reflects the country as it actually is: uneven, in motion, shaped by lived experience rather than abstract quotas. Samples are built with an understanding of the complexities which influence participation, yielding higher quality results.

Sector Field

Private & Custom Panels

When research requires a specific community — a region, a demographic, a membership base — Inquoro builds a dedicated panel for it. Sector Fields let organizations track sentiment within the audiences that matter most, over time. These segments can be examined in isolation, or alongside the general population — the Signal Field — to identify variance or alignment.

Field Relay

Text-to-Web Recruitment

Direct outreach via SMS, driving respondents to web-based surveys. Field Relay reaches people where life is already happening — with the speed and scale to field nationally or target precisely, from battleground districts to specialized populations.

Field Sense

Live Interviewer Surveys

Some research demands a human voice. Built in partnership with AMM Political Strategies, Field Sense deploys trained interviewers for phone-based collection — capturing nuance, building rapport, reaching the people digital channels miss.

Primary Signal

Custom Research, Built Around Your Mission

Primary Signal is Inquoro's core engagement: a research project designed from the ground up around your objectives, your audience, and the decisions that will be built upon our findings. Merging our Signal Field national panel, your Sector Field private panel, Field Relay text-based recruitment, and Field Sense live-agent telephone interviews, the Beamline seamlessly samples, recruits, fields, and weights — focused entirely by what you need to know.

The result isn't a generic survey report. It's intelligence built to your specifications: the right people, the right questions, data that connects directly to action. Single-wave or longitudinal. National or hyperlocal. Broad population or a segment no one else can reach.

Primary Signal is how questions become decisions.

Signal Scan

Omnibus Research

A regular sweep across the field. Signal Scan tracks public opinion on the issues that matter, month over month — building a longitudinal record that reveals not just where opinion stands, but where it's heading.

Resonance Detector

Ad & Campaign Effectiveness

Measures whether communications are actually changing minds. By identifying ad-exposed populations and comparing them against those outside the campaign's reach, Inquoro isolates real-world resonance.
